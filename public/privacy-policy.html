JustBlock Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 31, 2024

Introduction

JustBlock is a browser extension that helps users stay productive by blocking distracting websites. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our extension.

Information We Collect

When you use JustBlock, we collect the following information:

  • Email Address: We collect your email address when you sign in using Google Authentication. This is used to identify you as a user and determine whether you have a paid version for unlimited website blocking.

How We Use Your Information

We use the email address you provide for the following purposes:

  • To identify you as a user of JustBlock
  • To determine if you have a paid version and are eligible for unlimited website blocking
  • To communicate with you about your account or payment, if necessary

How We Protect Your Information

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Your email address is stored securely in our database and is only accessible to authorized personnel.

Third-Party Services

We use Google Authentication for user sign-in. When you sign in using Google, their privacy policy applies to the information they collect. We encourage you to review Google's privacy policy to understand how they handle your information.

Data Retention

We retain your email address for as long as you continue to use JustBlock. If you wish to delete your account and associated email address, please contact us at 7gorbachevm@gmail.com.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you by email or by posting a notice within the JustBlock extension.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us at 7gorbachevm@gmail.com.
